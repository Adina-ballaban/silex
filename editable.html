EGIDExpress offers researchers the opportunity to view published datasets generated by the Rothenberg CURED Laboratory on demand. Through this web-app, data can be easily accessed and readily visualized on a per gene basis or conveniently downloaded in bulk.

EGIDExpress is a platform to spur ideas and accelerate generation of hypotheses related to eosinophilic disease. Datasets will continue to be added as they become available.
